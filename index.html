Work Day Scheduler

A simple calendar app for scheduling your work day

How to use this app

  • Add your To-dos, by clicking on the input field next to the time slot of your choice.
  • Once you entered the task, click on the "save" icon to store it within the app.
  • You can edit specific task or simply delete it but most "save" again to update the app with the changes.
